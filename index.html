Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers before you hop in. If you still have questions, you can always ask in the Discord once you're inside.

What is No Sweat Bets?

No Sweat Bets is a private Discord community for sports bettors who want structured picks, live betting opportunities, and smarter bankroll management. We share research, tickets, and strategy – you always control what you bet.

How are picks and bets delivered?

All plays are posted directly in Discord channels with unit sizes and notes. You'll see pre-game cards, live entries when edges appear, and occasional long-term futures with clear notation.

What kind of bankroll do I need?

There's no minimum. We focus on percentage-based unit sizing so the framework works whether your unit is $10 or $1,000. The goal is to protect your roll and compound edges over time, not chase short-term heaters.

Do you offer refunds if I don't profit?

We can't guarantee profit or offer results-based refunds – no one in this space can honestly do that. Memberships are for access to information, education, and community. All payments and terms are handled securely through Whop.

Is this financial advice or a guarantee?

No. Nothing in the Discord or on this site is financial advice or a promise of any specific outcome. You're responsible for your own bets, limits, and decisions with sportsbooks.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes. Memberships are managed through Whop, where you can review your plan details and handle cancellations or changes according to their terms and timelines.
